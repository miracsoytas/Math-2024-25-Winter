## 2. Prove:

```math
\frac{d}{dx}(\ln(\sin(x))) = \cot(x)
```
<img width="662" alt="Ekran Resmi 2025-01-17 00 10 50" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d1113fac-407e-4d61-977b-747d86b54f33" />



### Proof:

Using the chain rule:

```math
\frac{d}{dx}(\ln(\sin(x))) = \frac{1}{\sin(x)} \cdot \cos(x) = \cot(x)
```
